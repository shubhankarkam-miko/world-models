The Decoupled Inference-Time Defense Loop

Neither a pure geometric filter nor a pure language-reasoning monitor can be both fast enough and safe enough on its own. The survey's synthesis pairs a semantically-blind, millisecond-scale Fast Reflexes loop with a reasoning-heavy, ~1Hz Slow Reasoning loop.
